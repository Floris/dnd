# Floryn - Skills & Feats Lookup

Quick reference for play. This file is based on your current sheet in `character.txt`.

## Your main skills

### Stealth (+10)
- Use this to hide, move unseen, or sneak past enemies quietly.
- This is one of your best skills.
- You have Expertise here, so your proficiency bonus is doubled.
- Useful for: ambushes, infiltration, escapes, and scouting.

### Sleight of Hand (+7)
- Use this for quick hand tricks and precise handwork.
- Useful for: pickpocketing, secretly taking or hiding something, manipulating small objects, setting up tricks, or any fine-fingered action.

### Perception (+6)
- Use this to notice things.
- Useful for: spotting hidden enemies, hearing sounds, noticing tracks or movement, and catching important details in a room.
- Your passive Perception is 16, so you notice a lot even without making an active check.

### Survival (+6)
- Use this in the wilderness or when tracking.
- Useful for: following tracks, navigating, judging danger, finding food, and reading signs from animals or nature.

### Investigation (+3)
- Use this to search logically and figure things out.
- This is different from Perception:
- Perception is noticing something.
- Investigation is understanding what it means or how it works.
- Useful for: finding hidden mechanisms, analyzing clues, and working out how something is put together.

## Other ability checks that may matter

### Athletics (-1)
- Climbing, jumping, pushing, pulling, and grappling.

### Acrobatics (+4)
- Keeping balance, moving neatly around someone, and agile movement.

### Charisma checks (+0)
- Deception, Intimidation, Performance, and Persuasion are all usable, but they are not your specialty.

## Your feats and fixed bonuses

### Alert
- You gain proficiency in Initiative.
- That is why your Initiative is now +7.
- You can also swap initiative with an ally if that is tactically better.
- Useful if you want to shoot after a frontliner engages first, or if a caster needs to act earlier.

### Archery
- You get +2 to attack rolls with ranged weapons.
- This is already included in your longbow attack bonus.
- It makes your longbow very reliable.

### Ability Score Improvement: Dexterity +2
- Your Dexterity increased from 16 to 18.
- That improves several things:
- your Initiative
- your ranged attacks
- your damage with finesse and ranged weapons
- your Dexterity skills
- your armor bonus within your medium armor limit

## Main ranger tools

### Longbow
- Attack bonus: +9
- Damage: 1d8 + 4 piercing
- Best choice at range.
- Because of Archery and high Dexterity, this is your most reliable attack.

### Shortsword
- Attack bonus: +7
- Damage: 1d6 + 4 piercing
- Useful if someone gets into melee with you.

### Extra Attack
- When you take the Attack action, you attack twice.
- With your longbow, that usually means two arrows each turn.

### Hunter's Mark
- Bonus action to mark a target.
- Very strong in longer fights or against tougher enemies.
- Good to cast early in combat on an important target.

### Hunter's Prey: Colossus Slayer
- Once per turn, you deal extra damage to a target that is already wounded.
- This works very well with your two attacks per turn.
- You usually want to shoot a target that has already taken damage.

### Hunter's Lore
- Gives useful information about a marked target.
- Helpful for quickly learning what an enemy is strong or weak against.

### Favored Enemy
- Hunter's Mark is always ready for you.
- You also get free casts per Long Rest.
- That makes you very efficient in combat.

### Roving
- Your speed is 40 ft as long as you are not wearing heavy armor.
- You also gain a climb speed and swim speed.
- Useful for positioning, kiting, and taking advantage of terrain.

## Good play pattern for Floryn

- Start fights at range with your longbow whenever possible.
- Use Stealth to begin from a good position.
- Put Hunter's Mark on an important target.
- Make two attacks with Extra Attack.
- Focus on enemies that are already wounded so Colossus Slayer gets value sooner.
- Use your high movement to keep distance and reposition smartly.

## Quick difference: Perception vs Investigation

### Perception
- "Do I see or hear something?"

### Investigation
- "What does this mean, and how does this work?"

## Quick difference: Stealth vs Sleight of Hand

### Stealth
- You are trying to stay unnoticed yourself.

### Sleight of Hand
- You are trying to do something secretly or precisely with your hands.

## One-line summary

Floryn is a fast ranged ranger who prefers to open fights from stealth, then keep pressure on wounded targets with longbow attacks, Hunter's Mark, and Extra Attack.
