BACKSTORY & PERSONALITY
-----------------------
The world is called Suhmara - a vast, lifeless planet covered in eternal
magical darkness. No sun, no stars, no dawn. In the skies above Suhmara,
a gargantuan castle-like structure known as "The Craydle" floats in low
orbit, glowing faintly like a pale, hollow moon.

This multi-levelled fortress is where you were born. It's where you live.
It's where you die. And it's all you know.

Inside, the Craydle is far from a traditional castle. Each floor is its own
world - some spanning the size of a small country, others no larger than a
village. Every floor has its own ecosystem, terrain, and inhabitants. Some
are wild. Some are civilized. Others are something else entirely.

At birth, every person receives a Coil - a magically conjured necklace that
appears without ceremony or explanation. Coils represent your power level or
potential. No one knows what conjures them, nor why, but their meaning is
universally accepted.

- Coils are removable and not bound to the person.
- They grant access, status, and in rare circles, function as a form of currency.
- The Coil you wear defines how others treat you.

The higher your rank, the higher your floor - and the greater your opportunities.
Each floor has its own laws, customs, and power structures. Some are ruled by
councils, others by warlords or guilds. Some floors are lawless wilderness.

But one truth holds everywhere:
Power is visible. Defiance rarely ends well.

Recently, rumors have stirred of Chainforged sightings on the lower levels.
The Chainforged are a guild known for their brutal codes and trial-by-combat
culture. Their presence here is unnerving - and no one knows why they've come down.

You begin your journey on Floor 7, a modest but established settlement known
as Braka. It is one of the first floors to support organized society: small
crafters, stable trade, and decently built housing for those ranked D2 and above.
The air carries the scent of perpetual spring - light forests, rolling hills,
and scattered wildlife stretch to the edge of this level.

The city is informally governed by a loose guild known as the Hollow Sigil,
who act as enforcers, mediators, and scouts for the surrounding region.

Braka's heart beats in The Cracked Coil, a worn tavern that hosts rumors,
drink, and contracts for those desperate enough - or strong enough - to take them.
